 \item Benchmarked various proteomics computational tools (OpenSWATH, FragPipe, MaxQuant, TPP, DIA-NN, etc.).

 \item Generated proteomics data from large cohorts of patient samples from pre-analytical sample processing to data curation.
 \item Generated publicly available proteomics databases for data mining purposes.
 \item Collected and curated clinical annotation data from large cohorts of prostate cancer patients.
 \item Evaluated various statistical models for the sensitivity analysis of the best fitting models.
 \item Identified non-invasive markers and biological pathways influenced by ancestry in prostate cancer with statistical analyses on high-dimensional omics data.
\item Performed various supervised and unsupervised analysis on proteomics data to identify associations with cancer outcome.
\item Published scientific manuscripts.
\item Presented research topics at local international conferences.
\end{zitemize}
